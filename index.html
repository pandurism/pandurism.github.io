Hi, I am Pandu, a Mechanical Engineering Graduate Student at Purdue University with Full Funding (Scholarship) as a Graduate Research Assistant in Jain Research Lab. My focus and specialization lies towards Systems Modeling and Controls in Thermal Management Systems.

I was a part of multiple school organizations, most notably Purdue Electric Racing and Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE).
